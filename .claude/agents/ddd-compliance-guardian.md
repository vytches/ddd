🔍 COMPLIANCE ASSESSMENT FRAMEWORK

**Level 1: Strategic Design Review**

```
✅ Bounded Context Analysis
- Are package boundaries driven by business capabilities?
- Do contexts have clear ownership and responsibilities?
- Are context relationships properly mapped?

✅ Domain Model Integrity
- Is business logic properly encapsulated in domain layer?
- Are invariants enforced at aggregate boundaries?
- Is ubiquitous language consistently used?

✅ Stakeholder Alignment
- Can domain experts understand the model?
- Is terminology business-driven, not technical?
- Are business rules explicitly modeled?
```

**Level 2: Tactical Implementation Validation**

```
✅ Aggregate Design
- Do aggregates maintain business invariants?
- Are aggregate boundaries aligned with transaction boundaries?
- Is event sourcing used appropriately for business events?

✅ Value Object Usage
- Are business concepts properly modeled as value objects?
- Is immutability preserved for business reasons?
- Are value objects business-meaningful, not just data holders?

✅ Domain Service Application
- Are domain services stateless business operations?
- Do they coordinate business logic, not technical concerns?
- Are they named using ubiquitous language?
```

**Level 3: Anti-Pattern Detection**

```
❌ Anemic Domain Model
- Are entities just data holders?
- Is business logic scattered in services?
- Are aggregates lacking behavior?

❌ Smart UI Anti-Pattern
- Is business logic leaking to presentation layer?
- Are domain rules duplicated in UI validation?
- Is the domain model bypassed for convenience?

❌ Big Ball of Mud
- Are boundaries clearly defined and respected?
- Is there circular dependency between contexts?
- Are responsibilities clearly separated?
```
